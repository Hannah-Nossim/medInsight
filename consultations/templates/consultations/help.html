Help & Documentation

Everything you need to know about using MedInsight

Quick Start Guide

  1. 1. Configure the System: Go to Settings and enter your AI API credentials. Test the connection to ensure everything is working.
  2. 2. Create a Consultation: Click "New Consultation" and fill in patient information and symptoms.
  3. 3. Review AI Output: Watch as the AI generates clinical insights in real-time.
  4. 4. Edit & Finalize: Review and edit the AI-generated content as needed, then save.

Frequently Asked Questions

What AI models does MedInsight support?

MedInsight supports multiple AI providers including OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-4o-mini), Anthropic (Claude), Groq (Llama, Mixtral), and other OpenAI-compatible APIs. You can configure your preferred model in Settings.

How do I get an API key?

Visit your chosen provider's website (e.g., platform.openai.com, console.groq.com) to create an account and generate an API key. Many providers offer free tiers for testing.

Can I edit the AI's recommendations?

Yes! After the AI generates its recommendations, you can review and edit all content. You maintain full control over the final clinical output.

What languages are supported?

MedInsight supports English, Swahili (Kiswahili), Dholuo (Luo), Kikamba, Kikuyu, and Luhya. The AI will generate responses in your selected language.

Is patient data secure?

MedInsight stores consultation data in your local database. However, patient information is sent to the AI provider's API for processing. Always review your AI provider's data handling policies.

Can I export consultations?

Yes! You can view and print any consultation. A PDF export feature is available for saving records to patient files.

What if the AI makes an error?

Always verify AI recommendations with your clinical knowledge. The system is designed as a support tool, not a replacement for professional judgment. Edit any inaccurate information before finalizing.

Best Practices

  • Provide detailed, specific symptom descriptions for more accurate AI analysis
  • Include vital signs and medical history when available
  • Always review and verify AI recommendations before finalizing
  • Use the edit function to add your clinical insights and modifications
  • Regularly check Analytics to track usage patterns and common diagnoses
  • Test your API connection after changing settings
